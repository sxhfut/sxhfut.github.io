<div class="research-lead">
  <h2><span class="lang-en">Research Mission: Define the Next Vocabulary of Affective AI</span><span class="lang-zh">研究使命：定义下一代情感智能的话语体系</span></h2>
  <p><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab studies how artificial intelligence can understand people more responsibly. Our work is organized around two conceptual frameworks we are deliberately defining: Ubiquitous Psychological Computing, which makes mental states computable across everyday environments, and Embodied Emotional Intelligence, which gives embodied agents the ability to perceive, understand, express, and interact emotionally. Together they form a research map from affective signals to psychological state, and from virtual models to embodied companionship.</span><span class="lang-zh">MAC-Lab 的研究不止于单点算法，而是主动围绕两条概念框架进行学术占位：普适心理计算，解决真实场景中心理状态如何被连续感知、建模、评估与干预；具身情感智能，解决机器人、数字人和智能终端如何具备情感感知、理解、表达与交互能力。两条框架共同构成从情感信号到心理状态、从虚拟模型到具身陪伴的研究版图。</span></p>
</div>

<div class="media-note">
  <h2><span class="lang-en">A Long-Term Route, Not a Hotspot Portfolio</span><span class="lang-zh">长期坚持方向，而不是追逐热点</span></h2>
  <p><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab's research grows from a stable foundation shaped by Professor Sun's doctoral training at Dalian University of Technology and Tokushima University: natural language processing, human-computer interaction, affective computing, and ubiquitous psychological computing. Large models, digital humans, smart cockpits, and embodied agents are not treated as isolated trends; they are new carriers that extend and deepen the same long-term question: how can AI understand human emotion, cognition, behavior, and psychological state more responsibly?</span><span class="lang-zh">MAC-Lab 的研究不是把每个新名词都做一遍，而是延续孙晓教授在大连理工大学和日本国立德岛大学形成的中日双博士训练底色，长期沿着自然语言处理、人机交互、情感计算、普适心理计算这条主线不断延伸和深化。大模型、数字人、智能座舱和具身智能不是孤立热点，而是承载同一个长期问题的新载体：AI 如何更负责任地理解人的情绪、认知、行为与心理状态。</span></p>
</div>

    <h2><span class="lang-en">Ubiquitous Psychological Computing</span><span class="lang-zh">普适心理计算框架</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">This framework extends affective computing from isolated emotion recognition to continuous psychological modeling in pervasive scenarios. It treats emotion as a dynamic process shaped by intensity, time, environment, personality, physiological-behavioral signals, and group context, enabling assessment, early warning, and personalized intervention.</span><span class="lang-zh">该框架把情感计算从孤立的情绪识别推进到普适场景中的连续心理建模：从情绪反应程度、时间、环境、个性、生理行为信号和群体情境等维度理解复杂情感变化，支撑心理评估、风险预警和个性化干预。</span></p>
    <div class="framework-axis">

    <h2><span class="lang-en">Embodied Emotional Intelligence</span><span class="lang-zh">具身情感智能框架</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">This framework brings affective computing into embodied agents. It connects multimodal emotional perception, affective understanding, expressive generation, and situated interaction so that robots, digital humans, smart cockpits, eldercare companions, and active-health systems can move from task execution to trusted emotional interaction.</span><span class="lang-zh">该框架把情感计算带入具身智能体：连接多模态情感感知、情感理解、情感表达生成与情境化交互，让机器人、数字人、智能座舱、养老陪伴和主动健康系统从“能执行任务”走向“能建立可信情感互动”。</span></p>

<div class="research-map">
  <section>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Affective NLP and Emotional Dialogue</span><span class="lang-zh">语言智能与情感对话</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">We develop models for Chinese language understanding, affective semantics, emotional conversation generation, empathetic response, personality-aware dialogue, and LLM-based emotional support. The goal is to help AI understand not only what is said, but also the emotional intent, social relationship, and psychological need behind the words.</span><span class="lang-zh">研究中文语言理解、情感语义、人格建模、情感支持对话和多智能体协作，让大模型不仅会回答，更能理解“为什么这样说”。</span></p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Multimodal Affective Computing</span><span class="lang-zh">多模态情绪与心理状态建模</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">We integrate text, speech, facial expression, micro-expression, gait, posture, eye movement, physiological signals, and environmental context. Research topics include complex emotion recognition, humor detection, stress estimation, depression and anxiety detection, cognitive load recognition, and temporal affect dynamics.</span><span class="lang-zh">融合文本、语音、面部表情、微表情、姿态、步态、眼动和生理信号，构建面向复杂情绪、心理压力、认知负荷和抑郁焦虑的识别模型。</span></p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Ubiquitous Psychological Computing</span><span class="lang-zh">普适心理计算</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">We propose and advance ubiquitous psychological computing: a framework for sensing, profiling, assessing, and supporting mental states in everyday scenarios. This direction emphasizes non-contact sensing, dynamic psychological modeling, long-term state tracking, early warning, and human-centered intervention.</span><span class="lang-zh">把心理状态感知从实验室带到真实空间，面向家庭、学校、医院、座舱和特种作业环境，形成非接触评测、动态画像、风险预警与干预闭环。</span></p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Embodied Emotional Intelligence</span><span class="lang-zh">具身情感智能</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">We study emotional interaction models and embodied agent architectures that act as an emotional brain for physical robots, digital humans, smart devices, and human-machine coexistence environments.</span><span class="lang-zh">研究具身情感交互大模型和智能体架构，为机器人、数字人、智能终端和人机共生环境提供可部署的“情感大脑”。</span></p>
  </section>
