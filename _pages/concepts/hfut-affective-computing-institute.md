<div class="card-grid card-grid--four">
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span>Foundation</span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Natural language processing</span><span class="lang-zh">自然语言处理</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Lexical analysis, parsing, machine translation, dialogue, language understanding, and affective semantics formed the early technical base.</span><span class="lang-zh">词法分析、句法分析、机器翻译、人机对话、语言理解和情感语义形成早期技术基础。</span></p>

  <article class="feature-card">

    <h3><span class="lang-en">Multimodal affective computing</span><span class="lang-zh">多模态情感计算</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Speech, face, text, behavior, physiology proxies, and scene information are treated as connected evidence for human state.</span><span class="lang-zh">语音、表情、文本、行为、生理代理信号和场景信息被作为理解人的状态的关联证据。</span></p>

    <h3><span class="lang-en">Ubiquitous Psychological Computing</span><span class="lang-zh">普适心理计算</span></h3>
