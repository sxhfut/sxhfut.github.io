  <h2><span class="lang-en">Definition</span><span class="lang-zh">定义</span></h2>
  <p><span class="lang-en"><strong>Embodied Emotional Intelligence</strong> is the MAC-Lab route for making affective AI usable inside bodies, devices, spaces, and long-term interaction. It links multimodal affect perception with emotional expression, response generation, cognitive regulation, memory, personality, and scenario constraints.</span><span class="lang-zh"><strong>具身情感智能</strong>是 MAC-Lab 将情感智能放入身体、设备、空间和长期交互中的路线。它把多模态情感感知与情感表达、回应生成、认知调节、记忆、人格和场景约束连接起来。</span></p>
</div>

<h2><span class="lang-en">From Perception to Situated Interaction</span><span class="lang-zh">从感知走向场景交互</span></h2>

<p><span class="lang-en">Emotion-aware systems are useful only when they can act appropriately in context. A robot, a digital human, or a smart cockpit cannot stop at recognizing a facial expression. It must understand task pressure, relationship, user history, communication style, safety boundary, and the cost of a wrong response. This is why MAC-Lab places embodied emotional intelligence after years of work on multimodal affective computing and psychological computing.</span><span class="lang-zh">能理解情感的系统，只有在具体场景中能做出合适反应，才真正有价值。机器人、数字人或智能座舱不能停留在识别一个表情，还要理解任务压力、关系、用户历史、沟通方式、安全边界和错误回应的代价。因此，MAC-Lab 把具身情感智能放在多年多模态情感计算和心理计算积累之后推进。</span></p>
