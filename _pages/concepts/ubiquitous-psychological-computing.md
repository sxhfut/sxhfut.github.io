<div class="concept-definition">
  <h2><span class="lang-en">Definition</span><span class="lang-zh">定义</span></h2>
  <p><span class="lang-en"><strong>Ubiquitous Psychological Computing</strong> is MAC-Lab's framework for bringing psychological computing into real environments. It combines multimodal affective computing, contextual sensing, longitudinal profiling, uncertainty-aware assessment, early warning, and human-reviewed support so that AI systems can understand human state as a situated, changing process.</span><span class="lang-zh"><strong>普适心理计算</strong>是 MAC-Lab 将心理计算带入真实环境的框架。它融合多模态情感计算、场景感知、长期画像、不确定性感知评估、早期预警和人在回路支持，使 AI 能够把人的状态理解为一个处在场景中、随时间变化的过程。</span></p>
</div>

<h2><span class="lang-en">Why This Concept Matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么这个概念重要</span></h2>

<p><span class="lang-en">A person's state is rarely explained by a single expression, sentence, or sensor value. It is shaped by time, task pressure, interpersonal context, personality, physiological rhythms, environment, and whether the person feels understood. Ubiquitous Psychological Computing gives MAC-Lab a way to connect these signals without reducing a person to a simple emotional tag.</span><span class="lang-zh">人的状态很少能由一个表情、一句话或一个传感数值解释。它受到时间、任务压力、人际处境、人格、生理节律、环境，以及“是否被理解”的感受共同影响。普适心理计算为 MAC-Lab 提供了一种连接这些信号的方式，同时避免把人简化为一个情绪标签。</span></p>
