<div class="research-lead">
  <h2><span class="lang-en">When emotion and human factors become the core of a scenario, a generic model is not enough.</span><span class="lang-zh">当情感与人因成为场景核心，通用模型就不够了。</span></h2>
  <p><span class="lang-en">Many partners begin with a seemingly simple need: recognize emotion, measure stress, detect fatigue, assess mental state, or improve human performance. In practice, these needs usually require a complete route: define the scene, select reliable signals, design assessment logic, model mind-body state, decide what support is appropriate, and deploy the system in a way that people can trust.</span><span class="lang-zh">很多合作方最初提出的需求看起来很简单：识别情绪、测量压力、检测疲劳、评估心理状态，或者提升身心效能。但真正做起来，往往需要一整条路线：定义场景、选择可靠信号、设计评价逻辑、建模身心状态、判断适合的支持方式，并把系统部署到用户能够信任的环境中。</span></p>
  <p><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab is built for this middle ground. The lab can provide algorithm modules, methods, platforms, and pilot validation, while keeping sensitive partner scenarios and internal technical details protected.</span><span class="lang-zh">MAC-Lab 的优势就在这段中间地带。实验室可以提供算法模块、方法体系、平台方案和试点验证，同时对合作方场景和内部技术细节保持必要保护。</span></p>
</div>

<h2><span class="lang-en">Scenario Tracks</span><span class="lang-zh">典型场景方向</span></h2>

<div class="project-list project-list--page">
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Smart Cockpit and Mobility Safety</span><span class="lang-zh">智能座舱与出行安全</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Driver and passenger state modeling requires more than fatigue detection. Emotion, stress, cognitive load, attention, trust, environmental context, and interaction timing must be considered together. MAC-Lab can support sensing routes, edge-deployable models, cockpit feedback logic, and evaluation protocols.</span><span class="lang-zh">驾驶员与乘员状态建模不只是疲劳检测，还涉及情绪、压力、认知负荷、注意、信任、环境情境和交互时机。MAC-Lab 可支持感知路线、边缘部署模型、座舱反馈逻辑和评测流程设计。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Education, Campus Mental Health, and Student Growth</span><span class="lang-zh">教育心理、校园健康与学生成长</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Campus scenarios need careful boundaries: screening, early warning, supportive feedback, competition training, learning-state analysis, and human-in-the-loop intervention. The lab emphasizes responsible assessment rather than mechanical labeling.</span><span class="lang-zh">校园场景需要边界清晰：筛查、预警、支持性反馈、竞赛训练、学习状态分析和人在回路的干预机制。实验室强调负责任评估，而不是机械贴标签。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Healthy Living and Active Health</span><span class="lang-zh">健康人居与主动健康</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Healthy living systems need to connect environmental variables with human mind-body state. MAC-Lab can help design measurement-evaluation-control routes that combine human factors, affective computing, active health, and platform deployment.</span><span class="lang-zh">健康人居系统需要把环境变量与人的身心状态连接起来。MAC-Lab 可协助设计融合人因、情感计算、主动健康和平台部署的测-评-控路线。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Public Safety and Mission-Oriented Human Performance</span><span class="lang-zh">公共安全与重大任务人因效能</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">High-stakes work needs evaluation methods that respect uncertainty. Stress, fatigue, risk, resilience, attention, decision behavior, and role-specific capability must be modeled with clear evidence boundaries and field validation.</span><span class="lang-zh">高可靠场景中的评价方法必须尊重不确定性。压力、疲劳、风险、韧性、注意、决策行为和岗位能力需要在证据边界清晰的条件下建模，并经过现场验证。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Digital Humans, Robots, and Embodied Emotional Interaction</span><span class="lang-zh">数字人、机器人与具身情感交互</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Embodied emotional intelligence is not about making a device appear emotional. It is about recognizing human state, choosing an appropriate response, controlling expression, remembering context, and staying safe in sensitive interaction.</span><span class="lang-zh">具身情感智能不是让设备“看起来有情绪”，而是识别人的状态，选择合适回应，控制表达方式，记住情境，并在敏感交互中保持安全边界。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Enterprise Mind-Body Capability and Role Fit</span><span class="lang-zh">企业身心能力与岗位适配</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">For training, management, service, and safety-critical roles, partners may need capability evaluation, cognitive-load monitoring, stress-risk analysis, and performance-support routes that are explainable and acceptable to users.</span><span class="lang-zh">面向训练、管理、服务和安全关键岗位，合作方可能需要能力评价、认知负荷监测、压力风险分析和身心效能支持路线，同时保证可解释、可接受。</span></p>
  </article>
</div>
