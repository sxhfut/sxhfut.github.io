<h2><span class="lang-en">Representative Capability Cases</span><span class="lang-zh">代表性能力案例</span></h2>

<div class="project-list project-list--page">
  <article>
    <h2>Mindmirror / SoulMirror</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Mindmirror is the lab's face-to-face AI mirror for guided psychological assessment. Its public value is to connect multimodal interaction, conversational exploration, and scale-informed psychological profiling into a reflective assessment experience. It is useful for showing how a system can read signals from the body, voice, expression, posture, and dialogue while still keeping the user in a respectful, interpretable, human-centered loop.</span><span class="lang-zh">Mindmirror 是实验室面向面对面心理评估的 AI 心镜能力线。公开表达的重点不是某个技术炫技点，而是把多模态交互、深度对谈和量表启发的心理画像组织成一种可解释、可复盘、对用户保持尊重的评估体验。它体现了系统如何综合身体、语音、表情、姿态和对话信号，同时保留人在回路中的理解与判断。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">multimodal assessment, camera and voice interaction, psychological profiling, reflective dialogue, report-level interpretation</span><span class="lang-zh">多模态评估、摄像头与语音交互、心理画像、反思式对谈、报告级解释</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">the public site describes the assessment route; model details, voice pipelines, prompts, databases, and deployment configuration remain protected</span><span class="lang-zh">公开主页只描述评估路线；模型细节、语音链路、提示词、数据库和部署配置不公开</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindScore</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindScore extends the assessment route into organizational settings. It is framed publicly as a mind-body growth guardian: a platform line for long-term psychological archives, non-contact multimodal sensing, baseline comparison, trend awareness, and intervention workflow. The emphasis is not a single score, but a responsible route from sensed evidence to growth support, risk awareness, and organizational care.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindScore 把面对面评估能力进一步延伸到组织场景，可公开表述为“心身成长守护平台”能力线：面向长期心理档案、非接触多模态感知、个体与群体基线对比、趋势感知和干预闭环。它的重点不是给一个简单分数，而是把可采集证据、成长支持、风险识别和组织关怀连接成负责任的路线。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">mind-body assessment, longitudinal profiles, baseline comparison, early awareness, intervention coordination</span><span class="lang-zh">身心评估、长期档案、基线对比、早期感知、干预协同</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">indicator philosophy can be shared; exact scoring formulas, constraints, model thresholds, and business workflow logic stay private</span><span class="lang-zh">可公开指标思想；具体评分公式、约束条件、模型阈值和业务流程逻辑保留</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindOS</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindOS is the lab's affective mind-computing foundation. Publicly, it should be understood as a research route for long-term embodied agents, psychological digital twins, and individual-to-group reasoning. The message is strong but restrained: emotional AI should have a stable mind-computing backbone, not only fluent language output.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindOS 是实验室的情感心智计算底座。对外可以理解为面向长期具身智能体、心理数字孪生和个体到群体推演的研究路线。公开表达要有力量但不展开配方：情感 AI 需要稳定的心智计算骨架，而不只是流畅的语言输出。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">affective mind modeling, embodied intelligence, psychological digital twins, long-term agent evolution, scenario reasoning</span><span class="lang-zh">情感心智建模、具身智能、心理数字孪生、长期智能体演化、场景推演</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">the concept and application route can be described; internal architecture, scenario packages, interfaces, evaluation assets, and implementation details remain private</span><span class="lang-zh">可描述理念和应用路线；内部架构、场景包、接口、评测资产和实现细节不公开</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindTalk</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindTalk is a simulation and training platform line built around individual-to-group psychological dynamics. It moves beyond single-session dialogue toward multi-domain inference: counseling and supervision training, psychological simulation, group emotion propagation, youth-support scenarios, strategy exploration, and research evaluation. Publicly, it demonstrates that emotional AI can be tested as a scenario system rather than judged only by whether one answer sounds empathetic.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindTalk 是围绕个体到群体心理动力学展开的推演与训练平台能力线。它不是只做单轮或单次对话，而是面向心理咨询与督导训练、心理仿真、群体情绪传播、青少年支持场景、策略探索和研究评估等多域推演。公开表达的重点是：情感 AI 需要在场景系统中检验，而不能只看某一句回答是否显得“共情”。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">psychological simulation, counseling and training scenarios, group affect propagation, intervention strategy evaluation, research dashboards</span><span class="lang-zh">心理仿真、咨询与训练场景、群体情绪传播、干预策略评估、研究看板</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">scenario types can be introduced; sensitive clinical logic, route design, data assumptions, and strategy optimization details stay controlled</span><span class="lang-zh">可介绍场景类型；敏感临床逻辑、路线设计、数据假设和策略优化细节保留在受控合作中</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindPet</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindPet is the lab's embodied companion-agent line. It explores how affective memory, personality-aware interaction, relationship awareness, and multimodal expression can make long-term companion systems more coherent and emotionally appropriate. Publicly, it shows that companion intelligence is not only friendly dialogue; durable trust requires continuity, restraint, and situated expression.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindPet 是实验室的具身陪伴智能体能力线，探索情感记忆、人格化交互、关系感知和多模态表达如何让长期陪伴系统更连贯、更合适。公开表达的重点是：陪伴智能不只是友好对话，长期信任需要连续性、克制感和场景化表达。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">companion agents, affective memory, personality-aware interaction, group affect, embodied expression</span><span class="lang-zh">陪伴智能体、情感记忆、人格化交互、群体情绪、具身表达</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">concepts and use cases can be shown; role design, state mechanics, user data, and behavioral policies stay private</span><span class="lang-zh">可展示理念和应用场景；角色设计、状态机制、用户数据和行为策略不公开</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MemOS-Mind</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MemOS-Mind is the lab's affective memory layer for long-term AI interaction. Its public idea is simple but important: a system should not only remember what happened; it should model what the event meant to the user and how the agent should respond through a stable persona and emotional stance. This capability supports companion AI, learning support, service assistants, and psychologically aware interaction.</span><span class="lang-zh">MemOS-Mind 是实验室面向长期 AI 交互的情感记忆能力线。它公开表达的核心很清楚：系统不应只记住“发生了什么”，还要理解这件事对用户意味着什么，以及智能体应如何以稳定人格和情感立场回应。这一能力可支撑陪伴 AI、学习支持、服务助手和具有心理感知能力的交互系统。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">affective memory, user-state modeling, persona-consistent response, long-term interaction continuity</span><span class="lang-zh">情感记忆、用户状态建模、人格一致回应、长期交互连续性</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">the memory philosophy can be explained; exact memory objects, weighting rules, prompts, and integration patterns remain private</span><span class="lang-zh">可解释记忆理念；具体记忆对象、权重规则、提示词和集成模式不公开</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

<h2><span class="lang-en">From Capability Pattern to Partner Solution</span><span class="lang-zh">从能力案例到合作方案</span></h2>

<div class="card-grid card-grid--four">
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Diagnose</span><span class="lang-zh">诊断</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Clarify the real problem behind the request</span><span class="lang-zh">找出需求背后的真实问题</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">A request for emotion recognition may actually be a need for safety warning, training evaluation, service improvement, mental-health support, or role-fit assessment.</span><span class="lang-zh">一个“情绪识别”需求，背后可能真正需要的是安全预警、训练评价、服务改进、心理支持或岗位适配评价。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Prototype</span><span class="lang-zh">原型</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Build a small route before exposing a large system</span><span class="lang-zh">先做小路线，再做大系统</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">The lab can help partners test signal feasibility, task definition, model route, user interface, and evaluation metrics before committing to full deployment.</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室可帮助合作方先验证信号可行性、任务定义、模型路线、用户界面和评价指标，再进入完整部署。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Validate</span><span class="lang-zh">验证</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Evaluate in the field, not only in a benchmark</span><span class="lang-zh">在现场验证，而不只看基准数据</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Field validation exposes sensor noise, missing data, privacy boundaries, user trust, edge constraints, and maintenance cost.</span><span class="lang-zh">现场验证会暴露传感噪声、数据缺失、隐私边界、用户信任、边缘约束和维护成本。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Transfer</span><span class="lang-zh">转化</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Leave reusable capability behind</span><span class="lang-zh">留下可复用能力</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">A good case should leave methods, modules, patents, evaluation protocols, student talent, and a clearer route for the next product iteration.</span><span class="lang-zh">好的案例应沉淀方法、模块、专利、评测流程、学生人才和下一轮产品迭代路线。</span></p>
  </article>
</div>
