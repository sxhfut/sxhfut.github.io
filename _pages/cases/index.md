<h2><span class="lang-en">Representative Capability Cases</span><span class="lang-zh">代表性能力案例</span></h2>

<div class="project-list project-list--page">
  <article>
    <h2>MindMirror</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindMirror represents MAC-Lab's route in multimodal psychological sensing and assessment. It combines facial, speech, behavioral, physiological, and contextual signals to support dynamic psychological profiling, screening, risk awareness, and personalized support. The public message is simple: psychological computing must move beyond one-time questionnaires and shallow labels toward continuous, multimodal, and scenario-aware evidence.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindMirror 代表 MAC-Lab 在多模态心理感知与评测方向的能力路线。它将表情、语音、行为、生理和情境信号结合起来，支持动态心理画像、筛查、风险感知和个体化支持。公开表达可以很清楚：心理计算不能停留在一次性问卷和浅层标签，而要走向连续、多模态、贴近场景的证据体系。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">non-contact sensing, multimodal fusion, psychological profiling, assessment workflow, human-in-the-loop validation</span><span class="lang-zh">非接触感知、多模态融合、心理画像、评测流程、人在回路验证</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">signal categories and scenario logic can be shared; data design, model details, and deployment architecture stay protected</span><span class="lang-zh">可公开信号类别和场景逻辑；数据设计、模型细节和部署架构保留在合作沟通中</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindOS</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindOS is the lab's forward-looking route for emotional and cognitive AI agents. It explores how large models can perceive affect, represent personality, regulate cognition, coordinate multiple agents, and provide emotionally appropriate support in long-term interaction. It is not presented as a single exposed technical system, but as a research foundation that can support digital humans, companion AI, psychological support, education, and human-machine coexistence.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindOS 是实验室面向情感与认知 AI Agent 的前瞻路线，关注大模型如何感知情感、表征人格、调节认知、协同多智能体，并在长期交互中提供合适的情感支持。它不作为单一暴露的技术系统展示，而是作为支撑数字人、陪伴 AI、心理支持、教育和人机共生的研究底座。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">affective LLMs, personality modeling, cognitive regulation, memory, multi-agent collaboration, support dialogue</span><span class="lang-zh">情感大模型、人格建模、认知调控、记忆、多智能体协作、支持性对话</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">framework and applications can be described; prompts, orchestration, model routing, and internal evaluation remain private</span><span class="lang-zh">可描述框架和应用；提示词、编排机制、模型路由和内部评测体系不公开</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindTalk</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindTalk represents the lab's capability in affective dialogue and emotional support. The value is not to make a chatbot sound warm in a few turns. The deeper problem is whether the system can understand affective intent, maintain boundaries, respond with restraint, recognize risk, and hand over to human support when needed.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindTalk 代表实验室在情感对话和情感支持方向的能力。价值不是让聊天机器人在几轮对话里“显得温暖”，而是系统能否理解情感意图、保持边界、克制回应、识别风险，并在需要时转向人类支持。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">affective NLP, empathetic dialogue, safety boundary design, support intent recognition, scenario adaptation</span><span class="lang-zh">情感自然语言处理、共情对话、安全边界设计、支持意图识别、场景适配</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">interaction principles can be shared; sensitive counseling logic and safety rules require controlled collaboration</span><span class="lang-zh">可公开交互原则；敏感咨询逻辑和安全规则需要在受控合作中讨论</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2>MindPet</h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MindPet is a public-facing way to describe companion interaction and embodied emotional support. It can connect affective perception, memory, personality, gentle behavioral feedback, and multimodal expression for companion agents, pet-like interfaces, robots, or digital life partners. The important point is that companionship is not decoration; it requires emotional appropriateness, safety, and long-term trust.</span><span class="lang-zh">MindPet 可以作为陪伴交互和具身情感支持的公开表达方式，连接情感感知、记忆、人格、温和行为反馈和多模态表达，面向陪伴智能体、类宠物界面、机器人或数字生活伙伴。关键在于，陪伴不是装饰，它需要情感合适性、安全边界和长期信任。</span></p>
    <dl><dt><span class="lang-en">Capability shown</span><span class="lang-zh">展示能力</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">companion agents, emotional memory, expressive generation, multimodal interaction, embodied support</span><span class="lang-zh">陪伴智能体、情感记忆、表达生成、多模态交互、具身支持</span></dd><dt><span class="lang-en">Public boundary</span><span class="lang-zh">公开边界</span></dt><dd><span class="lang-en">interaction concepts and scenarios can be shown; product logic, user data, and internal behavioral policies stay protected</span><span class="lang-zh">可展示交互概念和场景；产品逻辑、用户数据和内部行为策略保留</span></dd></dl>
  </article>

<h2><span class="lang-en">From Capability Pattern to Partner Solution</span><span class="lang-zh">从能力案例到合作方案</span></h2>

<div class="card-grid card-grid--four">
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Diagnose</span><span class="lang-zh">诊断</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Clarify the real problem behind the request</span><span class="lang-zh">找出需求背后的真实问题</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">A request for emotion recognition may actually be a need for safety warning, training evaluation, service improvement, mental-health support, or role-fit assessment.</span><span class="lang-zh">一个“情绪识别”需求，背后可能真正需要的是安全预警、训练评价、服务改进、心理支持或岗位适配评价。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Prototype</span><span class="lang-zh">原型</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Build a small route before exposing a large system</span><span class="lang-zh">先做小路线，再做大系统</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">The lab can help partners test signal feasibility, task definition, model route, user interface, and evaluation metrics before committing to full deployment.</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室可帮助合作方先验证信号可行性、任务定义、模型路线、用户界面和评价指标，再进入完整部署。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Validate</span><span class="lang-zh">验证</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Evaluate in the field, not only in a benchmark</span><span class="lang-zh">在现场验证，而不只看基准数据</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Field validation exposes sensor noise, missing data, privacy boundaries, user trust, edge constraints, and maintenance cost.</span><span class="lang-zh">现场验证会暴露传感噪声、数据缺失、隐私边界、用户信任、边缘约束和维护成本。</span></p>
  </article>
  <article class="feature-card">
    <span><span class="lang-en">Transfer</span><span class="lang-zh">转化</span></span>
    <h3><span class="lang-en">Leave reusable capability behind</span><span class="lang-zh">留下可复用能力</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">A good case should leave methods, modules, patents, evaluation protocols, student talent, and a clearer route for the next product iteration.</span><span class="lang-zh">好的案例应沉淀方法、模块、专利、评测流程、学生人才和下一轮产品迭代路线。</span></p>
  </article>
</div>
