    <h2><span class="lang-en">AI psychology is not emotion labeling</span><span class="lang-zh">AI 心理计算不是给情绪贴标签</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Emotion labels are useful, but they are too thin for real psychological computing. Human state is shaped by time, context, personality, language, body signals, social relationship, task pressure, and the person's willingness to be understood.</span><span class="lang-zh">情绪标签有用，但不足以支撑真实的心理计算。人的状态由时间、环境、个性、语言、生理行为信号、社会关系、任务压力以及“是否愿意被理解”共同塑造。</span></p>
    <dl>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab view</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室视角</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">Ubiquitous Psychological Computing should connect sensing, profiling, assessment, warning, and support, while keeping uncertainty visible.</span><span class="lang-zh">普适心理计算应连接感知、画像、评估、预警与支持，同时保留对不确定性的尊重。</span></dd>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">Why it matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么重要</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">A system that claims to understand people must avoid overconfident diagnosis, shallow affect labels, and one-size-fits-all intervention.</span><span class="lang-zh">一个声称理解人的系统，应避免过度诊断、浅层情绪标签和一刀切干预。</span></dd>
    </dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">The difficult middle between a paper and a deployed system</span><span class="lang-zh">论文到系统之间，最难的是中间地带</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">A model can work in a benchmark and still fail in a classroom, hospital, cockpit, counseling room, or home environment. Deployment exposes sensor noise, missing data, user trust, privacy boundaries, device constraints, and maintenance cost.</span><span class="lang-zh">模型可以在基准数据上有效，却未必能在学校、医院、座舱、咨询场景或家庭环境中稳定运行。真实部署会暴露传感噪声、数据缺失、用户信任、隐私边界、设备约束和长期维护成本。</span></p>
    <dl>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab view</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室视角</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">Research translation is a design discipline: define the task, data boundary, user role, evaluation protocol, and handoff mechanism before chasing model complexity.</span><span class="lang-zh">科研转化本身是一种设计能力：先定义任务、数据边界、用户角色、评测流程和交付机制，再谈模型复杂度。</span></dd>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">Why it matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么重要</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">Partners need more than a prototype. They need a team that can move through uncertainty without losing scientific discipline.</span><span class="lang-zh">合作伙伴需要的不只是样机，而是一个能在不确定性中保持科学纪律、工程判断和交付意识的团队。</span></dd>
    </dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">A good student competition should keep growing after the final</span><span class="lang-zh">好的竞赛项目，应该在比赛之后继续生长</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">Competition is a powerful entry point for undergraduate and master's students because it compresses problem definition, coding, presentation, teamwork, and scenario thinking into one cycle. But the medal is not the end of the research value.</span><span class="lang-zh">竞赛对本科生和硕士生很有价值，因为它把问题定义、代码实现、表达汇报、团队协作和场景思维压缩到一个周期里。但奖项不是科研价值的终点。</span></p>
    <dl>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab view</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室视角</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">Promising student work should be refined into papers, patents, software systems, datasets, reusable modules, and stronger research questions.</span><span class="lang-zh">有潜力的学生项目，应继续沉淀为论文、专利、软著、数据集、可复用模块和更好的研究问题。</span></dd>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">Why it matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么重要</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">This is how a lab turns student enthusiasm into durable capability instead of one-off demonstrations.</span><span class="lang-zh">这是一间实验室把学生热情转化为长期能力的方式，而不是停留在一次性展示。</span></dd>
    </dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Embodied emotional intelligence is about situated response</span><span class="lang-zh">具身情感智能，核心是场景化回应能力</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">The next step for AI is not only to answer correctly, but to respond appropriately in context. Robots, digital humans, smart cockpits, and companion agents must read multimodal signals, understand social timing, and express support with restraint.</span><span class="lang-zh">AI 的下一步，不只是回答正确，而是在具体情境中回应得合适。机器人、数字人、智能座舱和陪伴智能体，需要读取多模态信号，理解社会时机，并以克制、可信的方式表达支持。</span></p>
    <dl>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab view</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室视角</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">Embodied Emotional Intelligence connects perception, understanding, expression, action, safety, and long-term interaction memory.</span><span class="lang-zh">具身情感智能连接感知、理解、表达、行动、安全和长期交互记忆。</span></dd>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">Why it matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么重要</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">When AI enters health, education, mobility, and care, emotional appropriateness becomes part of technical reliability.</span><span class="lang-zh">当 AI 进入健康、教育、交通和照护场景，情感上的合适性本身就是技术可靠性的一部分。</span></dd>
    </dl>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h2><span class="lang-en">Why a stable research spine beats short-lived topic chasing</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么长期主线比短期追题更有力量</span></h2>
    <p><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab's route has moved from natural language processing to dialogue, affective computing, multimodal affective computing, ubiquitous psychological computing, and embodied emotional intelligence. The carriers changed; the core question stayed steady: how can AI understand and support human state?</span><span class="lang-zh">MAC-Lab 的路线从自然语言处理、人机对话、情感计算、多模态情感计算，延伸到普适心理计算和具身情感智能。载体在变化，核心问题一直稳定：AI 如何理解并支持人的状态？</span></p>
    <dl>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">MAC-Lab view</span><span class="lang-zh">实验室视角</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">A stable spine lets students, systems, papers, patents, platforms, and partners accumulate around one expanding problem space.</span><span class="lang-zh">稳定主线让学生、系统、论文、专利、平台和合作伙伴围绕同一个不断扩展的问题空间持续积累。</span></dd>
      <dt><span class="lang-en">Why it matters</span><span class="lang-zh">为什么重要</span></dt>
      <dd><span class="lang-en">This is the difference between following a trend and building a laboratory identity.</span><span class="lang-zh">这就是跟随热点和形成实验室身份之间的差别。</span></dd>
    </dl>
  </article>
